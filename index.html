NebulaBeat

The goal: Build a galaxy that reacts to your music — then let you share it with friends in real-time, across different networks, with zero lag. The challenge was combining WebRTC, Web Audio API, Canvas particle physics, and Socket.io into one seamless experience.

Real-time galaxy music visualizer — drop a song and watch the universe dance. Invite friends via QR code, share audio live over WebRTC, sync explosions and themes, AI genre detection changes the visual theme automatically, and record highlight clips on beat drops.
